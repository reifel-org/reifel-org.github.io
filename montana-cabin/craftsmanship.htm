    From start to finish, inside and out, this house has been built by a craftsman. In many cases hand-cut logs were used from the builder's other properties. Attention to detail is everywhere. Diamond insets and painted fascia trim the exterior and the hand-cut siding is lapped with dove-tailed joints. Throughout the house, the doors are works of art with hand-cut pine planks and walnut trim.
